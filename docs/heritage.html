Our Heritage

From Our Family to Your Table

Discover the rich history and tradition behind Laperla. Our olives are nurtured in family groves, handpicked, and pressed with care to create exquisite extra virgin olive oil.

The Laperla Story

For generations, our family has cultivated olive groves in the heart of the Mediterranean. Our journey began with a deep respect for nature and a passion for crafting exceptional olive oil. We have upheld the traditions of our ancestors while embracing modern techniques to ensure the highest quality in every bottle of Laperla.

Our Olive Groves

The Valerian Estate

Located in the sun-drenched hills of Valerian, this estate is the origin of our signature blend.

The Willow Creek Orchard

The Willow Creek Orchard, known for its diverse microclimate, produces olives with unique aromatic profiles.

The Cypress Hill Grove

Cypress Hill Grove's ancient trees yield olives renowned for their robust flavor and rich antioxidants.

Crafting Laperla

Our process begins with the careful selection of olives at their optimal ripeness. We employ a cold-press extraction method, preserving the natural flavors and nutritional benefits of the fruit. Each batch undergoes rigorous quality control, ensuring that only the finest oil bears the Laperla name.
