Health Benefits of Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Heart Health

Rich in monounsaturated fats, EVOO lowers bad cholesterol and reduces heart disease risk.

Anti-Inflammatory

Polyphenols in EVOO reduce inflammation, easing chronic conditions like arthritis.

Antioxidant Power

Antioxidants like vitamin E protect cells from damage, supporting overall health.

Digestive Health

EVOO promotes healthy digestion and may reduce the risk of digestive disorders.

Cognitive Benefits

Healthy fats in EVOO support brain health, potentially lowering cognitive decline risk.

Skin Health

EVOO’s antioxidants and fats nourish skin, reducing aging signs when used topically or consumed.
